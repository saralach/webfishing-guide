Fish

Freshwater Fish

Freshwater fish can be found in the lakes and ponds scattered around the island.

Saltwater Fish

Saltwater fish can be found in the ocean that surrounds the island.

Rain Fish

Rain fish can only be caught while it is raining.

Miscellaneous Fish

Miscellaneous fish can only be found in a variety of unusual ways that are specific to each fish.

Junk Items

On occasion, you may fish up a junk item rather than a fish. Your odds of catching these items is increased by using the Magnet Lure.
